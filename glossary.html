Lighting Glossary

Find all our lighting technologies explained here in detail

White LED Strips

As a base, we offer white LED strips in cool white, warm white, and neutral white
Our White-Only LED strips are available in Standard and Addressable(featured), meaning
control of individual LEDs is possible leading to a wide variety of possible effects.



Standard RGB Strips

The most classic option in colorful lighting! The color on these strips can be controlled
however, the entire strip must remain the same color all throughout. These are reliable and
a great starting place for entering a colorful future.



Addressable RGB Strips

Do you want the best lighting effects available? Then you require the power of Addressable LEDs!
Each LED is inidivually controllable and with our Spectacle Lighting Control Module, you can create
a beautiful canvas of light on any surface. Our best seller!


Spectacle Lighting Control Module at work!



We have combinations and variations!

Maybe you'd like having the best of both worlds? We offer LED strips with BOTH RGB and White in the
the same space, what could be better? We have specialty light strips that can acheive any look you want.
